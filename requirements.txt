Requirements / Desired Features

Interface & integrate nicely with IS4C/Fannie
Store, edit & view basic member information
Store, edit & view detailed member information
Accessible from almost any computer on intranet
Store, edit & view payments to members
Track payments to members
Make threaded notes and replies to notes about members
Search for members by last name, first name, or member number
Automatic activation, deactivation of members
Auto-reminder before & after payment due date
Mass mailer for list subscribers
Mailing label printing (Avery standards)
Phone list printing by various criteria
Printing folder labels
Basic & detailed reporting
Link to Fannie transaction/payment records
Authentication / basic user levels
Detail, basic versioning
Export and import backups
Automatic nightly backups
